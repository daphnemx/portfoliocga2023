System Monitoring Dashboard

Tools | Technologies | Methodologies:
sketchInVISIONcarbon design system react html sass
Overview

Our internal engineering team lacked an efficient and centralized system to monitor and oversee critical system processes. The purpose was to provide a user-friendly and visually appealing interface that would streamline the monitoring of system processes from the apps the technical members of the team developed and maintained, and allow them to be aware of any potential issues with the ongoing tasks.

Research and findings

The applied strategy consisted on conducting user interviews and analyzing the obtained feedback. The findings revealed the need for a continual visual representation of the systems status and intuitive controls for efficient monitoring and maintenance. The system monitoring did not have a visual interface (the users were running processes on the terminal).Additionally, it was crucial to effectively transition the existing experience flow to the new interface design.

Design, testing and iteration

I created the prototypes using Sketch and applying IBM Design principles to ensure that the outcome will align with the brand identity. I also utilized IBM's Carbon Design System, which brought consistency and maintainability to the final result. The design aimed to provide a visually appealing and consistent experience for the team. Emphasis was placed on clear information display, intuitive navigation, and accurate representation of the user flow found on the research phase.

To validate the designs, I conducted multiple usability testing sessions with end users. Their feedback was crucial in refining the interface and aligning it with their specific needs. Iterative feedback loops were employed to address usability concerns and ensure that the design met their expectations.

Implementation

In addition to my role as a UX strategist, I had the opportunity to implement the code for the dashboard's homepage. Leveraging my knowledge of the Carbon Design System and React JS, I translated part of the finalized design into functional code. This allowed for a seamless integration between the design and development process.

Results and key learnings:

Throughout this project, I learned the importance of involving coworkers as end users and aligning the final result with their needs. By conducting user interviews and validation sessions with the internal engineering team, I gained valuable insights that shaped the design process. Additionally, having the opportunity to implement part of the code and collaborate closely with the development team improved overall work efficiency, resulting in a streamlined digital dashboard that effectively met the team's requirements.
