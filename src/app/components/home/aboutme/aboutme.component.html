Meet me

I became passionate about design at an early age when I discovered the power of inanimated things to evoke emotions through interaction. I strive to be the architect of well-designed products that blend user needs with business goals to help companies deliver consistently successful products.

My BFA studies in Graphic Design provided me a strong foundation in visual design. As I explored web development, I got excited when I could translate my website designs into code (I coded this portfolio using Angular). This naturally led to merge my skills with user interface design. My recent 3+ years experience designing digital products at IBM played a significant role in shaping my professional career as a product designer.

I posses strong skills as a UX Strategist, Frontend Developer, and Product Designer.

  • User research
  • User interface design
  • Front-end development
  • Usability testing
  • Visual/Graphic design
  • Video editing

I believe great design should be grounded on reality , so empathizing with the user is fundamental in each creation process. As it is rooted in human psychology, good design is timeless and perfectible, a designer's work is never truly done, it is adaptable to changes and sometimes it leads the change. And finally I believe design should be enjoyable, it evokes positive emotions and bring delightfulness to the context where it is meant to exists.
