Meet me

I became passionate about design at an early age when I discovered the power of inanimated things to evoke emotions through interaction. I strive to be the architect of well-designed products that blend user needs with business goals to help companies deliver consistently successful products.

My BFA studies in Graphic Design provided me a strong foundation in visual design. As I explored web development, I got excited when I could translate my website designs into code (I coded this portfolio). This naturally led to merge my skills with user interface design. My recent four year experience designing digital products at IBM played a significant role in shaping my professional career as a product designer. My goal is to specialize in designing meaningful experiences that bring practical value and make a tangible impact on people's everyday lives. Besides design, I'm passionate about playing the piano and ukulele, indulging in reading, drawing, and take walks with my dog.

I possess strong skills as a UX Strategist, Frontend Developer, and Product Designer.
